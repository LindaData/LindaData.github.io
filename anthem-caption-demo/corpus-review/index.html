How the next loop should run

1. IngestCollect open-license ceremony clips, assign stable `video_id`, and log source provenance once.
2. EvidenceRead title prior, audio fingerprint, pitch contour, melody/chroma, and tone shape for every audio-ready row.
3. DecisionUse a combined score to infer anthem identity and the likely anthem window, then queue low-confidence rows for review.
4. Caption swapWhen confidence is strong, replace normal ASR output with curated anthem lyrics and the user-selected translation.
