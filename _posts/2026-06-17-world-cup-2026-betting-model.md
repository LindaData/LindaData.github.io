title: 2026 World Cup Betting Model
---

This project is a reproducible modeling workflow for the 2026 FIFA World Cup. It uses R, Python, SQL, DuckDB, and Quarto to organize public data, fit baseline models, and publish a GitHub Pages report.

Current reports include:

- Team goals linear regression.
- Ordinal logistic regression for loss, draw, and win.
- Local refresh options for rebuilding the analysis as new data arrives.
