I am expanding Linda Data into a shareable team resource for statistical analytics, regression modeling, model tuning, LLM evaluation, diagnostics, and reproducible project examples.

The new workbench is designed around real analyst workflows: choose the question, copy the starter, swap in the data, run diagnostics, and keep the exhaustive catalog in the background until it is needed. It includes:

- A regression catalog covering linear, generalized linear, regularized, panel, causal, time-series, survival, spatial, Bayesian, multivariate, and machine-learning model families.
- A 100-row statistical technique catalog that covers inference, experimentation, resampling, ANOVA, nonparametric tests, causal inference, time series, survival, multivariate methods, clustering, reliability, Bayesian analysis, survey analysis, meta-analysis, and LLM evaluation.
- A pre-model diagnostics checklist for collinearity, heteroskedasticity, missingness, outliers, dependence, leakage, overdispersion, zero inflation, and assumption-specific checks.
- A data pantry centered on R built-in datasets, plus no-key public APIs and key-based APIs worth adding.
- Starter R, Python, and SQL codebooks that can be reused across future projects.
- A focused sample library for analysts who want to swap in their own data and column names quickly.
- A model tuning workbench for validation design, stepwise functions, decision trees, XGBoost, random forests, regularization, calibration, ensembles, LLM tuning, and model cards.
